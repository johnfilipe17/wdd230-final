A little about us

We are a small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, however, we have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Our company sells fruits and produce juice and desserts directly from the farmers in the area. All of our products are fresh. Our farms are awesome and most of them work with robots operated by humans what avoid heavy labor for them.
We delight in seeing our employees working with a smile on their faces, so they can give their best and produce what is healthy and fresh with love and self-respect. We belive in equality, health, honesty and respect to our costumers and employess.
